Raid Protection

Giveaways

Hold giveaways for your members with the giveaway command, featuring a custom countdown timer, completely ad-free.

Music

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? These are some of our most frequently asked questions yo help you out!
What does premium do?
By purchasing Premium you support development of Maki and unlock extra features to improve your overall experience. A list of key features can be found above!
On how many servers can I use premium?
Premium can only be used on one server. If you wish to unlock the premium features on multiple servers, you have to buy it again.
Can I pay with another currency?
Yes! At checkout, Paypal will tell you the transfer rate and how much premium costs in your currency. Everything happens automatically!
Can I transfer my premium to another server?
This is currently not possible. Please contact us if you accidentally purchased premium for a wrong server.
How do I cancel a Premium subscription?
You can find an overview of your subscriptions and a way to cancel them on the profile page.
How can I support development even more?
There are a ton of ways you can help out! Things like boosting the support server and donations are greatly appreciated! You can apply for staff too! Join The support server for more information.
Where can I find my invoice?
An invoice will be sent to the email address bound to your PayPal account.
When will my premium be activated?
Premium activates almost instantly after a successful purchase!
